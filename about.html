Hi, I'm Eddie.

I'm a voice and ukulele teacher in the San Francisco Bay Area, and I work with singers and performers who want to stop overthinking and start feeling their music.

I didn't start music young. I came to it as an adult—after a decade in tech. I know what it's like to feel behind, uncertain, and like you're doing everything wrong. I also know what it's like to find your voice anyway.

My Teaching Combines

  • Somatic practice — Connecting to your body so your voice can be free
  • Classical technique — Bel canto breath support, posture, resonance
  • Black American music traditions — Soul, gospel, jazz—the music I grew up with
  • Performance coaching — Because singing in your bedroom is different from singing on a stage

I'm an off-the-cushion Zen Buddhist, which means I bring mindfulness and nervous system awareness into my teaching. If you get anxious before performing, if you feel disconnected from your voice, if you want to sing like you mean it—I can help.
